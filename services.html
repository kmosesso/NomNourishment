Program Options

Everyone is different, so all packages are customizable to suit your individual needs. All programs include personalized handouts, virtual pantry clean-up, recipes, e-mail support between sessions, and progress tracking.

Prices vary based on the customized plan. Monthly payment plans available.

Sign up for a complimentary 30-minute discovery call to review your health goals and to discuss which, if any, program is right for you. No commitment required!

1) Customizable individual coaching: Whether you feel super motivated to dedicate time to your health and you know how to hold yourself accountable, but just need some help sorting through all that confounding nutrition information or you need a serious jumpstart and have no clue where to start, I meet you where you are and we create a plan together.

2) Customizable group and corporate sessions: ideal for a group of friends or family with similar health goals or for companies dedicated to the health and wellness of their employees. All program sessions are conducted via audio and video platforms.

3)Customized weekly meal suggestions: Add on to any coaching program

Come on, take a ride...

Nourishment is about more than the food you put in your mouth. There are many lifestyle factors that need to interact symbiotically to form a nourished, healthy individual: nutritious food, regular movement, job satisfaction, fulfilling relationships, spirituality, stress management, creativity, adequate sleep and regularly doing the things you love. The body is a powerful, integrative machine capable of self-healing when provided with these conditions.

Kara is a board certified nurse practitioner, certified holistic health coach, distance runner and digital nomad. She uses evidence-based science to empower YOU to make sustainable lifestyle changes in order to improve overall wellness and prevent, better manage and even reverse chronic disease. She believes health and wellness are about abundance, expanding your vision and vitality, not dieting and deprivation.
